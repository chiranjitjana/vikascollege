Principal message

Matoshri Parvati Raut Vidyalaya established in the year 2010 completes 7years this year. We started two classes Jr.Kg. & Sr. Kg. and now we arerunning 8 classes. Jr. Kg., Sr.Kg. and 1st to 6th Std.the total number of students studying in all 260. The number of staff includingnon-teaching staff is 21. The status of our school is self finance EnglishMedium School. The school conducts various curricular and co-curricularactivities. Students activtely take part in various competitive exams such asInternational Olympiad for Science, Mathematics, and English and Ganit SambodhPariksha & Ganit Pravinya Pariksha for 5th Std. organized bySindhudurg Ganit Adhyapak Mandal.

We arranged Annual Sport Day every year and students take part in it withgreat enthusiasm. From last three year we started cub-bulbul & scout-guideactivity. Last year students attended taluka level scout-guide camp in (Malvan– Poiep ) and have achieved success. They got many prizes in competitions likesushobham and group song. This year also students attended District Level Campin Malvan (Masure) and had succeeded with total 14 prizes in differentcompetitions like Camp Fire, Knotting, shobhayatra etc. This year StudentsParcticipated in Taluka level Science Exhibition and achieved 1stPrize.

Any chievement or success cannot be single handed for our success andcredit goes to our teachers for their dedicated service during school hours andbeyond. Also the support and guidance from Shri. P. M. Raut Sir, Mrs.RautMadam, Shri. Vinay Sir, Shri. Vikas Sir & Shri. Mohan Vast Sir the founderand authorities of our institution.

Mrs. Sandhya Prabhu
Head Mistress
