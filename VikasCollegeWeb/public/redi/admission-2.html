Admission Process for Std 5

In the 2nd week of April admission forms are printed and distributed to the existing Std 4 students of Udayachal Primary School. Parents are required to submit the filled forms along with the documents on the dates mentioned for submission.

The documents which are required are as follows :Original Leaving CertificateCopy of Birth CertificateProof of AddressCopy of Pan Cards of parentsCopy of student’s Adhar Card

Do note that presence of the candidate and parents at the Introduction Session is mandatory. Parents are also required to be physically present at the school to complete the admission process, if the child is selected for admission.

None of the system-allotted dates will be postponed or advanced, hence requests for the same will not be entertained. Age criteria cannot be relaxed under any circumstances.

Further, detailed information about the admissions process, including fees schedule and bus stops, will be updated on this website closer to the opening date for Registrations.

The school reserves the right to alter the schedule, and any other information given on this website, without any prior notice, if necessary to do so.
